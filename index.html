About me

Who am I?

Hey there! I have a passion for maths and computer science, and i am always eager to dive into new learning experiences and push my boundaries. I am actively searching for a job so please don't hesitate to reach out!

My background

My academic journey started with two and a half years at INSA (National Institute of Applied Sciences) in Toulouse, France – a pretty prestigious engineering school. As I went along, I gravitated towards computer science, largely because I've always had a thing for coding and playing around with math problems. I got to mix things up with an ERASMUS exchange semester at Linköping University in Sweden, which was a real game-changer in opening up my perspective.

Skills & Expertise

My programming journey began back in middle school when I started tinkering with Python and algorithms. In high school, I even got into a nationwide programming competition and ranked 14th. While doing my engineering studies, I kept busy with personal projects that helped solidify my understanding. At university, I dove into all sorts of topics, from operating systems to web development and much more. I'm not just about theory – I've also tackled hands-on projects that have sharpened my skills and given me a well-rounded toolkit.
